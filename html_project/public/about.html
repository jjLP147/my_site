About Me


Originally from the United States, I chose adventure over stability at home.

After learning professional-level Japanese during and through university, the infamous COVID-19 struck and shut down my accepted job as well as the whole world.

I then looked for other opportunities and was introduced to South Korea by my pastor and his wife.

Seeing the opportunity to learn another language and culture even in the middle of the pandemic, I took an opportunity to come to South Korea and have been working here since.
