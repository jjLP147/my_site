3 of My Favorite Films I Saw in the Past Year

Jason Weaver

The Northman

This movie will immerse you in the mindsets and cultures of 1000A.D. Northern Europe.
As a man of Norse descent and a fan of ancient history and the so-called "Viking Age,"
this movie spoke to me on levels most films cannot.

The film follows Prince Amleth in his quest for vengeance against his father's killer.

Schindler's List

This should need no introduction; it is frequently cited on Best Movies of All Time lists,
expecially those surrounding WWII.

This movie follows one Oskar Schindler, a National Socialist Party member
in 1930s-1940s Germany. He tries to keep Jewish people safe from the Third Reich
under the guise of using them for labor.

How to Train Your Dragon

A fun break from the seriousness of the other two movies;
this one follows the antics of a Viking lad named Hiccup and his journey
to gain respect and renown in his homeland.
